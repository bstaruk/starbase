starbase

starbase is an open source webpack 3, Babel (ES6) & PostCSS boilerplate for web developers, with a small footprint and plenty of headroom for making it your own.

  • why come?
  • what does it do?
  • who is it for?

starbase was created in early 2017, when webpack 2 was released and it became clear that many popular development boilerplates were not being maintained or upgraded to the latest versions of the software they were built to utilize.

The primary mission of starbase is to provide an elegant, but simple baseline development process that generates clean, production-ready assets while being bloat-free and easy to maintain.
